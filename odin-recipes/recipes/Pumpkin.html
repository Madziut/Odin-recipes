Paul's Pumpkin Bars

These easy pumpkin bars are full of fall flavor with a moist texture and a decadent cream cheese frosting. So far, I haven't found anyone who doesn't love them!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Pumpkin puree
  • Sugar
  • Vegetable oil
  • Flour
  • Cinnamon
  • Baking soda
  • Baking powder
  • salt

Steps

  1. Bake the cake
  2. Make the frosting
  3. Frost
  4. Cut into bars
